How to play: This is a two player game; on player uses the arrow keys to move the tiles and tries to merge them while the other player uses the mouse and clic on the empty cells on the grid to make appear a '2' tile or a '4' tile. The first player's goal is to create a '2048' tile and the other player has to prevent him to reach it.
Use the left clic to put a '2' tile and the right clic to put a '4' tile. The player putting the tiles is the one who starts; he plays twice at the begining of the game.

Note: This site isn't the official version of 2048. You can play it on http://git.io/2048. All other apps or sites (like this on) are derivatives or fakes, and should be used with caution.
